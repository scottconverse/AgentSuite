PatentForgeLocal exists because patent drafting is one of the few high-stakes activities still gated by access. If you're an inventor in a town with no patent attorney, you either pay $4,000 for a provisional, ship something the USPTO will reject, or stay quiet. PatentForgeLocal is the third path. We run on your laptop. We do not call home. We do not collect your invention disclosure. The model lives in your Ollama install. The drafts live on your disk. We are deliberately small. We do not promise to revolutionize anything. We promise to draft a defensible provisional from a one-page disclosure on a $700 ThinkPad without an internet connection.
